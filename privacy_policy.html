CV GIME Privacy Policy

Welcome to CV GIME. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our professional CV creation application.

Information We Collect

CV GIME does not require authentication or any login. All information you enter—names, email addresses, resume details—is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or retain any personal data on external servers.

How We Use Information

Because your data never leaves the app, we do not use it for any external purposes. The information is used solely within the application to create and manage your CVs.

Data Security

All data resides locally; its security depends on your device. Please ensure your device is protected and consider backing up your files responsibly.
